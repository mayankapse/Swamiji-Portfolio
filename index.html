I am

I have been associated with The Art of Living since 2009, taking the vow of Brahmacharya in 2012 under the guidance of Gurudev Sri Sri Ravi Shankar Ji. I have played a pivotal role with the VDS (Vaidic Dharma Sanathan), its various activities and also for the many sister organizations and initiatives along with the Art of Living. I have been teaching various Art of Living programs like Happiness Program, Sahaj Samadhi Meditation, DSN, YES!+, etc. I have conducted several programs in India and abroad. I have been instrumental in conducting several programs for the Indian Army, Indian Navy, and Indian Air Force. I have been a part of the team that conducted the first-ever Happiness Program in the Indian Parliament.
