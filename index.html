Swami Brahmachaitanya Ji has been associated with the Art of Living since 2009, taking the vow of Brahmacharya in 2012 under the guidance of Gurudev Sri Sri Ravi Shankar Ji. He has been teaching various Art of Living programs like Happiness Program, Sahaj Samadhi Meditation, DSN, YES!+, etc. He has conducted several programs in India and abroad. He has been instrumental in conducting several programs for the Indian Army, Indian Navy, and Indian Air Force.
